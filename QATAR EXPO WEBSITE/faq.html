FAQs

What is Qatar Expo 2023?

The initial horticultural global expo in a desert enclosure, uniting art, technology, and plant growth enthusiasts. A celebration of horticultural innovation and eco-friendly living.

What is the aim of Expo 2023?

1.Host the first desert climate horticultural exhibition.
2.Foster global cooperation on environmental, cultural, and financial matters.
3.Tackle the worldwide challenge of Desertification.
4.Promote horticultural knowledge sharing for Qatar National Vision 2030 and encourage innovative horticulture in Qatar's climate.
5.Applying the Green City Concept in the Middle East.

What is the plan of Expo 2023?

The Al Bidda site will have three zones:
1.International Area: Entrance with global gardens and events.
2.Family Area: Hub for family activities and outdoor fun.
3.Cultural Area: Perfect for cultural activities, near historical Doha.
Post-expo, it becomes a World Cup fan zone and Doha Grand Park.
